# AI-Exam-Tutor-RAG
A professional RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipeline built with Python 3.11, Streamlit, and FAISS. Features a dual-mode system: a Live PDF Uploader and a Pre-Trained Q&amp;A bot grounded in historical and AI ethics datasets.

This version is optimized for your **Bookxpert Pvt. Ltd.** internship submission. It includes:
* **Project Overview**: Explicitly mentions the AI Engineering Intern assignment.
* **Dataset Details**: Highlights the specific 5 documents you used (WWII, UNESCO AI Ethics, and the Transformers paper).
* **Folder Structure**: Matches your local "my project" directory.
* **Dual-App Description**: Explains the difference between your live uploader (`app.py`) and the pre-trained version (`app2.py`).
* **Technical Justification**: Includes your choice of sentence-based chunking and strict grounding strategies.
